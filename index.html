About me

Hi, I'm Siebe Boeckx and I'm a Gameplay Programmer.
I love designing systems that have a direct impact on gameplay or the designing of levels.
I work best when given certain tasks or problems and I'm constantly trying to learn new methods to produce more efficient and cleaner code.

Plunder 'n Pints
This was a Game-Jam project that I completed with another developer and 4 artists.
I was responsible for all the trap functionality, making them as modular as possible so someone else could focus on the level design. Furthermore, I was also responsible for the camera system including the respawning when a player leaves view.
Lastly it was my responsibility to make sure all the collisions of the assets were accurate.
Amber the Witch
This was a Game-Jam project that I completed with 5 other developers, it was also my first time creating something in VR.
My responsibilities were the interactive book and it's visuals, the recipes themselves, the task board and then hand-in + pointing system.
